Houdini Foundation

Duration: 6 weeks

This 6-week beginner track builds practical Houdini fundamentals, from interface fluency to procedural thinking, basic simulation, and small production-ready outputs.

Learning Goals

  • Understand node-based workflows and data flow logic.
  • Build procedural modeling setups with attribute control.
  • Create reusable scene structures with clean organization.

6-Week Curriculum

  • Week 1: Houdini UI, node graph basics, SOP context, and file discipline
  • Week 2: Procedural modeling fundamentals with Group/Transform/Copy workflows
  • Week 3: Attribute fundamentals, randomness, and controlled variation
  • Week 4: Intro to VOP/VEX and simple parameter-driven setup design
  • Week 5: Basic simulation intro (particles/RBD), cache concept, and scene cleanup
  • Week 6: Final mini project, critique session, and next-step study roadmap

Final Deliverables

  • One procedural modeling output
  • One basic simulation shot
  • Node network captures with short process notes

Recommended For

  • Students and artists starting Houdini for the first time
  • Creators with DCC experience who are new to procedural workflows
  • Learners preparing for Master or Procedural Generation tracks
